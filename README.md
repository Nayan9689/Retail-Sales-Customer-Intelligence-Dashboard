Retail Sales Analysis Using Python, SQL & Power BI
📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales performance to understand revenue trends, product demand, customer purchasing behavior, and store‑level performance. Using Python, SQL, and Power BI, the goal was to uncover insights that help retailers optimize inventory, pricing, and marketing strategies.

🎯 Problem Statement
Retail businesses often struggle with fluctuating sales, unpredictable demand, and inefficient inventory planning.
This project aims to answer:

Which products drive the most revenue

How sales vary across time, categories, and customer segments

What factors influence high or low sales performance

How to improve forecasting and business decisions

🛠️ Approach & Tools
Python (EDA + Data Cleaning)
Cleaned and prepared transactional sales data

Analyzed product‑wise, category‑wise, and store‑wise performance

Identified seasonal and monthly sales patterns

SQL (Data Structuring & Querying)
Aggregated sales by product, region, and time

Identified top‑selling and low‑performing items

Segmented customers based on purchase frequency and spend

Power BI (Interactive Dashboard)
Built visualizations for revenue, profit, and category performance

Created drill‑downs for product, region, and time analysis

Designed KPIs for sales, profit margin, and growth trends

🔍 Key Insights
Certain product categories consistently drove the highest revenue

Sales peaked during specific months, indicating strong seasonality

A small percentage of products contributed to the majority of revenue (Pareto pattern)

Some regions underperformed despite high footfall, suggesting pricing or stock issues

High‑value customers showed predictable repeat‑purchase behavior

💼 Business Impact
The insights help retail teams:

Optimize inventory for high‑demand products

Improve pricing strategies for low‑margin items

Plan seasonal promotions more effectively

Identify underperforming stores and fix operational gaps

Strengthen customer retention through targeted offers

📈 Outcome
This project demonstrates how data analytics can transform raw retail data into actionable insights that improve revenue, profitability, and customer satisfaction. It highlights end‑to‑end skills in EDA, SQL querying, KPI modeling, and dashboard storytelling.
